What does it mean to be conscious?

The definition of consciousness is quite simple. At it's simplest, consciousness is sentience or awareness of internal and external existence.

While this concept may be a simple one, we can go minutes, hours, days, weeks, months, sometimes even years without being aware of our internal and external existence.

It can be very easy to get caught up in the whirlwind of circumstances we call our lives, in which case our consciousness becomes hijacked.

Luckily, there are practices that we can implement into our daily routines that can give us access to reclaiming our consciousness.

One of the most popular and effective practices to regain control of our consciousness is meditation.

Meditation

Meditation is a practice where an individual uses a technique – such as mindfulness, or focusing the mind on a particular object, thought, or activity – to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm and stable state.
